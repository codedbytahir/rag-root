# RAGroot Product Demo Script

## Brand Identity
- **Product Name:** RAGroot
- **Tagline:** Grounded Intelligence for the Enterprise.
- **Primary Color:** #39E079 (Grounded Green)
- **Secondary Color:** #0bcbcb (Cyan Flow)
- **Font:** Geist (Sans-serif)
- **Logo:** /public/logo.png (Network icon)
- **Tone:** Professional, minimal, high-tech, and secure.

## Demo Script Section

### Scene 1: The Entry
- **Location in app:** \`/login\` (LoginPage)
- **What to show:** The high-energy glass auth card and the 3D glassy left panel with SOC2 and Zero Latency badges.
- **Camera motion:** Slow zoom into the "Continue with Google" button.
- **Duration:** 5s
- **Voiceover:** "Welcome to RAGroot. The enterprise-grade RAG platform that turns your data into grounded intelligence."
- **Motion graphic overlay:** "Secure. Private. Verifiable."

### Scene 2: The Workspace
- **Location in app:** \`/dashboard\` (Dashboard)
- **What to show:** Pulse skeleton loaders transitioning into the list of active Brains. Highlight the "New Brain" button.
- **Camera motion:** Pan across the brain cards, focusing on the status badges.
- **Duration:** 6s
- **Voiceover:** "Manage multiple isolated knowledge bases effortlessly. No more data leakage between projects."
- **Motion graphic overlay:** "Isolated Brain Architecture"

### Scene 3: Building Knowledge
- **Location in app:** \`/brain/[id]\` (Brain Detail Page)
- **What to show:** Uploading a PDF. Show the "AI is Vectorizing" loading state.
- **Camera motion:** Zoom into the upload progress indicator.
- **Duration:** 7s
- **Voiceover:** "Upload your documents and let our high-precision engine vectorize them in seconds."
- **Motion graphic overlay:** "HNSW Optimized Vector Indexing"

### Scene 4: Tailored Intelligence
- **Location in app:** \`BrainSettings.jsx\` (Settings Modal)
- **What to show:** Opening settings, selecting a new LLM model from the live list, and editing the system prompt.
- **Camera motion:** Pan from the LLM selector to the System Prompt textarea.
- **Duration:** 8s
- **Voiceover:** "Customize each brain with specific LLM models and tailored system prompts to fit your exact business needs."
- **Motion graphic overlay:** "Live Model Fetching & Custom Persona"

### Scene 5: Integration Ready
- **Location in app:** \`/brain/[id]\` (Sidebar)
- **What to show:** Clicking the "Copy Brain ID" button. Show the "Copied!" confirmation.
- **Camera motion:** Macro shot of the Copy button.
- **Duration:** 4s
- **Voiceover:** "Integration is a click away. Copy your Brain ID and power your external apps through our robust Public API."
- **Motion graphic overlay:** "Developer-First Integration"

### Scene 6: Verifiable Answers
- **Location in app:** \`ChatInterface.jsx\` (Chatting with Brain)
- **What to show:** Asking a question and seeing the response stream with citation links.
- **Camera motion:** Follow the text as it streams, then zoom into a citation source.
- **Duration:** 10s
- **Voiceover:** "Get answers you can trust. Every response is grounded in your documents with verifiable citations."
- **Motion graphic overlay:** "100% Grounded. Zero Hallucinations."

## Recommended Tools
- **Screen recording:** Screen Studio (for automatic zooms and pans).
- **Motion graphics:** After Effects or Jitter.video for Apple-style typography.
- **Voiceover:** ElevenLabs (using a professional, deep, slightly technical male or female voice).
- **Export settings:** 4K, 60fps, HEVC (H.265) for maximum clarity.
